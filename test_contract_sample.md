# SERVICE AGREEMENT

**This Service Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026, by and between:

**SERVICE PROVIDER:** TechSoft Solutions Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 123 Business Park, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400001, India ("Service Provider")

**CLIENT:** Global Industries Corporation, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 456 Corporate Avenue, Bangalore, Karnataka 560001, India ("Client")

---

## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Service Provider shall provide the following services to the Client:

1.1 Cloud Infrastructure Management Services including but not limited to:
- Provisioning and configuration of cloud servers
- Network infrastructure setup and management
- Database administration and optimization
- Security monitoring and patch management
- Performance tuning and optimization

1.2 The Services shall be provided in accordance with the specifications and requirements outlined in Appendix A (Statement of Work), which is attached hereto and forms an integral part of this Agreement.

1.3 The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to deliver the Services in a professional and timely manner.

---

## 2. TERM AND COMMENCEMENT

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on January 15, 2026 ("Commencement Date") and shall continue for a period of two (2) years, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

2.2 The Agreement may be renewed for additional periods of one (1) year each, upon mutual written consent of the parties.

2.3 Either party may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days' written notice to the other party.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

3.1 **Service Fee:** The Client shall pay the Service Provider a monthly fee of INR 500,000 (Five Hundred Thousand Indian Rupees) for the Services rendered.

3.2 **Payment Terms:** All invoices shall be issued on the 1st day of each month for the services provided during that month. Payment shall be made within 15 days of invoice receipt to the Service Provider's designated bank account.

3.3 **Taxes:** All fees mentioned above are exclusive of applicable GST (Goods and Services Tax). The Client shall be liable for all applicable taxes.

3.4 **Late Payment:** If payment is not received within 15 days of invoice date, a late fee of 1.5% per month shall be charged on the outstanding amount.

3.5 **Annual Price Revision:** The Service Provider reserves the right to increase the service fee by up to 10% annually, effective from the anniversary date of this Agreement, provided 30 days' notice is given.

---

## 4. SERVICE LEVELS AND PERFORMANCE

4.1 **Uptime Commitment:** The Service Provider commits to maintain a minimum of 99.9% uptime for the services provided, measured on a monthly basis.

4.2 **Support Hours:** Technical support shall be available 24/7 (24 hours a day, 7 days a week).

4.3 **Response Times:**
- Critical Issues: 1 hour response time
- High Priority: 4 hours response time
- Medium Priority: 8 hours response time
- Low Priority: 24 hours response time

4.4 **Maintenance Windows:** The Service Provider may conduct scheduled maintenance during the following windows:
- Weekdays: 10:00 PM to 2:00 AM IST
- Weekends: Anytime (with prior notice)

4.5 **SLA Credits:** If uptime falls below 99.9%, the Client shall be entitled to service credits as follows:
- 99.0% to 99.9%: 5% service fee credit
- 98.0% to 99.0%: 10% service fee credit
- Below 98.0%: 25% service fee credit

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 Each party agrees to treat all confidential information of the other party with strict confidentiality and not to disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

5.2 Confidential information includes but is not limited to source code, documentation, business plans, customer lists, and proprietary technology.

5.3 This obligation shall survive for a period of three (3) years after termination of this Agreement.

5.4 The receiving party may disclose confidential information only to its employees, contractors, and advisors who have a legitimate need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5.5 Exceptions: The obligations under this clause do not apply to information that:
- Is already in the public domain
- Was rightfully possessed by the receiving party before disclosure
- Is received from a third party without confidentiality restrictions
- Is independently developed without using the confidential information

## 7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

7.1 **Limitation of Liability:** Except in cases of willful misconduct or gross negligence, neither party shall be liable to the other for:
- Indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages
- Loss of profits, revenue, or business opportunity
- Loss of data or business interruption

7.3 **Indemnification:**

## 8. DATA PROTECTION AND SECURITY

8.1 **Data Backup:** The Service Provider shall perform daily backups of all critical data maintained on the Client's behalf.

8.2 **Data Security:** The Service Provider commits to maintaining industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Incident response procedures

8.3 **Data Breach Notification:** In case of any data breach or unauthorized access, the Service Provider shall notify the Client within 24 hours.

8.4 **Data Deletion:** Upon termination of this Agreement, the Service Provider shall securely delete all Client data within 30 days, unless required by law to retain it.

8.5 **Compliance:** The Service Provider shall comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and India's data protection laws.

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

9.2 **Jurisdiction:** Both parties agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

9.3 **Dispute Resolution Process:**
- **Step 1:** Any dispute shall first be addressed through good faith negotiations between senior representatives of both parties (within 15 days)
- **Step 2:** If unresolved, the dispute shall be referred to mediation (within 30 days)
- **Step 3:** If mediation fails, the matter shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996

9.4 **Arbitration:** A single arbitrator shall be appointed by mutual consent of the parties. The arbitration shall be conducted in English at Mumbai.

## 10. TERMINATION CLAUSE

10.1 **Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:
- The other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice
- The other party becomes insolvent or bankrupt
- The other party engages in illegal activities

10.2 **Termination for Convenience:** Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason with 60 days' written notice.

10.3 **Effects of Termination:**
- All services shall cease on the termination date
- The Client shall pay all outstanding invoices
- The Service Provider shall return or delete all Client data as per Section 8.4
- Obligations regarding confidentiality and IP shall survive termination

10.4 **Survival:** Sections 5 (Confidentiality), 6 (Intellectual Property), 7 (Liability), and 9 (Dispute Resolution) shall survive any termination of this Agreement.
